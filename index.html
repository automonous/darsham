Darsham Services Hub

The team of the Darsham Services Hub (DSH) works with farmers and landowners in East Suffolk, wishing to diversify and help to meet the housing needs of Sizewell C. We help create custom housing solutions by leveraging industry relationships, expertise and collective purchasing. Our project management extends to deliver operational support for leasing periods and ongoing maintenance and hotel operations.

The People Behind
Darsham Services Hub

The Darsham Services Hub has been established by Philip Evans & Jane Blore.

Philip Evans

Co-Founder

After training and experience working in the hotel industry, Philip established PEX an international provider of software services and accommodation management solutions for residential and student housing providers. Philip is also a local hotelier.

Jane Blore

Co-Founder

Jane who is supported by her sons Brodie & Buster have been local farmers and land owners for many years. Jane is also a Managing Director — Head of Asset Management UK for Martley Capital. She oversees asset management across all sectors of the commercial and alternative real estate market and is the Investment Adviser for Alternative Income REIT.
